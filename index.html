★Longing for Connection: A Floral Fantasy 🌼 div.

I had a flower in my hand and wished that I could tuck it in your hair.


Your hair, like a field of wheat, dances in the breeze and glows in the sun.


It would be the perfect place for this flower to rest, nestled among your locks.


I imagine you smiling as I place it there, your eyes shining with appreciation.


Perhaps someday you will come out of my imagination and I'll have the courage to tuck that flower in your hair and tell you how I feel.


Until then, I'll keep this bloom as a reminder.
